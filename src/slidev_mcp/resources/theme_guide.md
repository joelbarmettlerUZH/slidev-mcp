# Theme Selection Guide

Choose a theme based on the presentation's purpose and desired aesthetic. After selecting, read `slidev://themes/{name}` for the theme's full documentation — layouts, components, and frontmatter options.

## By Category

### Dark Themes
- **neocarbon** — Premium cinematic dark theme. Best-in-class for pitch decks. 22 layouts, glassmorphism effects, animated chart components, ambient backgrounds with particles and gradients.
- **dracula** — Classic Dracula color scheme. Simple and familiar for developer audiences.
- **vibe** — Neon-accented dark theme with glassmorphism and floating particles. Eye-catching for tech demos.
- **the-unnamed** — Subdued dark theme based on the VS Code dark palette. Good for code-heavy presentations.
- **cobalt** — Deep cobalt blue with framed slide layouts. Professional feel for data presentations.

### Academic / Formal
- **academic** — Built for lectures and research. Supports footnotes, citations, and slide numbering.
- **scholarly** — LaTeX Beamer-style formatting. Ideal for thesis defenses and paper presentations.
- **frankfurt** — Beamer Frankfurt-style with navigation bar. Structured and familiar for conference audiences.
- **neversink** — Education-focused with whimsical touches. Flexible admonitions and teaching aids.

### Modern / Minimal
- **geist** — Vercel design language. Ultra-clean typography, perfect for developer and product talks.
- **eloc** — Writing-focused minimalism. Strips away decoration to let content shine.
- **default** — Slidev's built-in theme. Zero configuration, standard layouts.
- **apple-basic** — Apple Keynote-inspired. Clean, professional, no frills.

### Playful / Creative
- **purplin** — Vibrant purple with gradient accents. Energetic for startup and creative pitches.
- **unicorn** — Colorful with dark/light modes. Portfolio and design presentation friendly.
- **bricks** — Bold, colorful brick-pattern layouts. Stands out at workshops and meetups.
- **shibainu** — Warm, friendly tones with rounded elements. Approachable for community talks.

### Retro / Unique
- **field-manual** — Vintage 1950s-1980s US Army manual aesthetic. 30+ layouts, distressed textures. Highly distinctive.
- **nord** — Arctic-inspired muted palette. Calm, focused, great for design-conscious audiences.

### Professional / Corporate
- **penguin** — Blue accents with clean header/footer. Presenter info built in. Reliable for team updates.
- **swiss-ai-hub** — Swiss AI-Hub branded with gradient backgrounds. Purpose-built for AI/tech organizations.
- **zhozhoba** — Versatile dark/light theme. Clean design that works for any audience.
- **vuetiful** — Vue.js green branding. Natural fit for Vue/open-source ecosystem talks.

## Choosing Tips

- **"Modern and sleek"** — Try `geist`, `neocarbon`, or `vibe`
- **"Professional and clean"** — Try `apple-basic`, `penguin`, or `seriph`
- **"Academic or research"** — Try `academic`, `scholarly`, or `frankfurt`
- **"Dark and dramatic"** — Try `neocarbon`, `dracula`, or `vibe`
- **"Fun and creative"** — Try `purplin`, `unicorn`, or `bricks`
- **"Unique and memorable"** — Try `field-manual`, `nord`, or `neocarbon`
- **"Simple, just works"** — Try `default` or `seriph`
- **"Feature-rich with components"** — Try `neocarbon` (25+ components) or `field-manual` (30+ layouts)
